Homepage | Vietnam-Quiz

What is the capital city of Vietnam?

Hanoi

Which river is significant in Vietnamese culture and flows through both Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City?

The Red River (Sông Hồng).

What is the traditional Vietnamese noodle soup known for its flavorful broth, rice noodles, and various toppings?

Pho

Which famous historical site in Vietnam served as the political and administrative center of ancient Vietnamese emperors?

The Imperial City of Hue.

What is the traditional Vietnamese celebration that marks the Lunar New Year and involves various festive activities and special foods?

Tet Nguyen Dan (or short "Tet").

Which UNESCO World Heritage site in Vietnam is famous for its stunning limestone karst formations and is a popular destination for boat tours?

Ha Long Bay

What is the currency used in Vietnam?

Vietnamese Dong (VND)

How many Vietnamese Dong transfer to 1€?

about 25.000 VND

Which Vietnamese dish is a savory pancake made with rice flour, water, and turmeric powder, filled with ingredients like shrimp, bean sprouts, and herbs?

Banh Xeo

What does the famous Dragon-Bridge in the city of Da Nang do every night?

spit water and fire
